Project Goals:

  • NSS Chronicles is a cli-application that is intended to provide an interface to a database of current and former NSS students.
  • Perform CRUD on cohorts, students and student projects.
  • Experiment with one to many and many to many relationships in SQL and Active Record.

  • Refactoring with Blocks.
